Stay in sync with your pulse wherever you want, Open Pulse connects instantly to any BLE‑compatible heart rate strap or watch and shows you real‑time data at a glance.

Key features:
- Live heart rate display: BPM reported from your device in real time.
- Battery indicator: See remaining battery on your sensor, so you don't get caught unprepared.
- Bluetooth Low Energy connectivity: Fast, low-energy pairing with most popular heart-rate monitors.
- Open Source & Transparent: The app's source code is freely available on GitHub, so it can be reviewed by anyone, and you can contribute.
- Non-intrusive ads: Optional banner ads at the bottom of the screen, UI designed around them. Don't like them? Just say no.
- Simple UI: Clean, distraction-free interface to make it easy for you to see what you need during your workout.

How it works:
- Turn on Bluetooth and launch Open Pulse
- Scan for nearby heart‑rate sensors with Bluetooth LE
- Tap your device to connect
- Monitor your BPM and battery levels

The app remembers your selected device, detects issues with connectivity, reports them, and will automatically try to reconnect.
